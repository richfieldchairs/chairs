Introduction

Bergen Abbon Woven Dining Chair is a unique piece thanks to the one-off tones and grain of the solid wood. It is sourced from sustainable forests using controlled felling methods.

Features

A stylish and modern wooden chair, the Bergen Abbon Woven Dining Chair is ideal as a set for the dining table, or single as an occasional seat. She features a curved backrest that extends down to her slender legs. Coupled with a wide and smooth seat surface that will give you ample seating space, Abbon gets you comfortable when entertaining guests at the dinner table. Abbon Dining Chair features four wood layers of varnish for durability and the chair and woven seat have been given an anti-UV treatmen.
