5 Mid-Century Modern Sofas to Breathe Life Into Your Living Space

Break into the timeless design trend statement pieces from Kardiel

JANUARY 04, 2022

For the home-decor-obsessed, a scroll through your Instagram feed and a browse through a tasteful design store will have one thing in common: plenty of mid-century modern furniture. Year after year, the timeless style dominates decor and design lists, proving its tasteful simplicity has graduated from a trend to a more permanent fixture—and it's with good reason. Professional designers and amateurs alike are drawn to the clean lines, organic curves, and contrasting materials used in this iconic style. If you’re looking to spruce up your space with an elegant mid-century modern flair, look no further than Bergen and Bel Air, two of our favorite small-batch furniture brands.

Founded by Josh Smith, who shares a passion for modernism and architecture, Bergen's sofas are hand-designed and constructed with only the highest quality materials. Think leather from Milan, wood from Australia, and textiles from top-tier international design houses. Whereas, Bel Air’s sofas seem more contemporary, with textures resembling both traditional and modern values.

When styling a home with mid-century modern pieces, it is important to gradually build your design so as to not overwhelm the space. That is why you should always start with the pièce de résistance of any living area: the sofa. From a retro-inspired multifunctional daybed to a cloud-soft bouclé sectional, here are a few of our favorite mid-century modern sofas from Bergen to get you started.

Founded by Josh Smith, who shares a passion for modernism and architecture, Bergen's sofas are hand-designed and constructed with only the highest quality materials.
The Bergen Miro is perfection in contemporary design.
1. BERGEN MIRO

The Bergen Miro is perfection in contemporary design. Made with the sturdiest of materials, its deep seats with well-cushioned back and armrests exudes a classic Danish design while resting on slim metal legs to ensure that this sofa retains its visually light profile despite its firm support.

This sofa has been upholstered with SMART fabrics by Davis, which features an easy clean, water repellent fabric to allow you that small window of time to swipe liquids up quickly.

2. BEL AIR ORSON

Bergen Orson is a stunning example of modern Scandinavian design. Featuring streamlined carved walnut legs and a minimalist shape with attention drawing button detailing, the Orson is a statement piece for any home.

For the assembly, the metal frame is put together - completely without screws and tools - and then stretched with the fabric cover. The matching upholstery and cushions are placed on it and already the sofa is assembled. A flexible and lightweight design that is easy to transport and move. A design that is "simple, relaxed and for everyone" according to the designers Ronan and Erwan Bouroullec.

Bergen Orson is a stunning example of modern Scandinavian design.
Bel Air Branagh is filled with high density foam which provides you with maximum comfort and support
3. BEL AIR BRANAGH

Sitting nicely on angled legs in walnut stain, Bel Air Branagh is filled with high density foam which provides you with maximum comfort and support. Upholstered from 100% polyester, her seat cushion covers are removable for fuss-free maintenance. Available in 2 colours - Raven and Dusk Blue and 3 versions - 3 Seater, Loveseat and Armchair, she is perfect to complement your Scandinavian living room

The frame of the Branagh is made of pine wood, which can be painted in different colours. On the frame is a Basic Futon mattress. The filling of the futon consists of 4 layers of cotton and a 4 cm thick foam core. The cover, which is available in various attractive colours, is made of polyester and cotton. In addition, two matching cushions are included.

4. BEL AIR ELMIRA

The Elmira Right Hand Facing Corner Sofa is a stylish contemporary sofa designed for lounging. Featuring super-deep seats that allow you to lie almost all the way back (throw cushions optional), this modern sofa also comes with a low profile that will enhance the sense of space in your living room.

This is a sectional sofa consisting of individual units arranged together. Sofa units can be re-configured to suit individual needs and preferences. This chaise sofa can be used in the living, study or bedroom to provide extra seating spaces. However, 2 pieces of this sofa can be creatively combined to create an L Shaped sofa or a spacious 4 seater sofa that is great for large living rooms. Available in Granite and Stone and separately as a chaise lounge or together as a 4 seater sofa.

You can choose from Right or Left Arm Chaise Unit:

Right arm unit: When facing the arm unit, the armrest is on the right.

Left arm unit: When facing the arm unit, the armrest is on the Left.

The Elmira Right Hand Facing Corner Sofa is a stylish contemporary sofa designed for lounging.
The Bergen Sete Accent Armchair is a luxuriously comfortable lounge chair.
5. BERGEN SETE

The Bergen Sete Accent Armchair is a luxuriously comfortable lounge chair, made using the highest quality faux fur. In other words, luxurious guilt-free fun. It combines both beauty and function with the exposed wood at the top of the chair back.

The space also creates a functional element of being able to move the chair by using the exposed wood parts instead of by touching the upholstered fabric of the back. The tightly upholstered inside back features double-needle stitching at the outer edges. The tightly upholstered arm has double-needle stitching along the inner edge that flows into the double-needle stitching of the inside back. Exposed wood seat rails frame the base of the chair seat. The lightly scaled, tapered exposed wood legs are supported on the sides and across the back by stretchers.
