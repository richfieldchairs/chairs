Introduction

The Elmira Right Hand Facing Corner Sofa is a stylish contemporary sofa designed for lounging. Featuring super-deep seats that allow you to lie almost all the way back (throw cushions optional), this modern sofa also comes with a low profile that will enhance the sense of space in your living room.

Features

This is a sectional sofa consisting of individual units arranged together. Sofa units can be re-configured to suit individual needs and preferences. This chaise sofa can be used in the living, study or bedroom to provide extra seating spaces. However, 2 pieces of this sofa can be creatively combined to create an L Shaped sofa or a spacious 4 seater sofa that is great for large living rooms. Available in Granite and Stone and separately as a chaise lounge or together as a 4 seater sofa.

You can choose from Right or Left Arm Chaise Unit:
• Right arm unit: When facing the arm unit, the armrest is on the right.
• Left arm unit: When facing the arm unit, the armrest is on the Left.
